About Me

I'm a Data Scientist at X.AI Inc. Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Research Scientist at Columbia University. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer and Information Science at University of Delaware in 2014.

I am broadly interested in the field of information retrieval, natural language processing and machine learning. Specifically, I am interested in the study and development of effective and efficient evaluation techniques that help us measure how well search systems satisfy user needs.

Thus far, I have conducted research on these topics and worked on the following research problems: evaluation of retrieval, question answering systems that have applications in a variety of domains, including web search, recommendation systems, and healthcare. As a result of this work, I created open-source tools, contributed to commercial software and published in leading academic conferences.
